Nice

Highlights

  • Promenade des Anglais and pebbled beaches
  • Old Town (Vieux Nice) with markets and cafés
  • Easy access to nearby coastal villages and Monaco

1-day sample

  1. Morning - Cours Saleya market and breakfast
  2. Afternoon - Walk the Promenade des Anglais, beach time
  3. Evening - Sunset viewpoint at Castle Hill (Colline du Château)

Local tip

Try socca (chickpea pancake) from a local vendor - a regional specialty.
