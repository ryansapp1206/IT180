Tokyo

Highlights

  • Shibuya Crossing and neon-lit skyscrapers
  • Asakusa (Old Town) with temples and street food
  • Easy access to Mount Fuji and coastal Kamakura

1-day sample

  1. Morning – Tsukiji Outer Market and fresh sushi breakfast
  2. Afternoon – Explore Senso-ji Temple and Nakamise-dori
  3. Evening – Sunset views from the Tokyo Skytree or Shibuya Sky

Local tip

Try monjayaki (savory pancake) in Tsukishima - a regional Tokyo specialty.
