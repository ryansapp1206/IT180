Tokyo

Highlights

  • The Tokyo Skytree and panoramic city skylines Senso-ji Temple
  • The traditional markets of Asakusa Neon-lit streets

1-day sample

  1. Morning - Starts with "Old Tokyo" culture while the crowds are thinner.
  2. Afternoon - Visit the serene forest of Meiji Jingu.
  3. Evening - Ends with the quintessential "Modern Tokyo" experience and world-class dining.

Local tip

Get a welcome Suica or Pasmo card at the airport—it’s a tap-to-pay card that works for all trains, buses, and even vending machines.
