Tokyo

Highlights

  • The Great Boiling Valley (Owakudani) and sulfurous steam vents
  • Lake Ashi cruises with views of the "floating" torii gate
  • The Hakone Open-Air Museum and its mountain-framed sculptures

1-day sample

  1. Morning - Ride the Hakone Ropeway over volcanic valleys to Owakudani.
  2. Afternoon - Take a sightseeing "pirate ship" across Lake Ashi toward Hakone-jinja Shrine.
  3. Evening - Relax in a traditional onsen (hot spring) and enjoy a multi-course kaiseki dinner.

Local tip

Purchase the Hakone Free Pass—it covers your round-trip from Tokyo and gives you unlimited access to the local trains, cable cars, ropeways, and boats.
