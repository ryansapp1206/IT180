Personal Interests

When I am not studying, I spend a lot of my time exploring new hobbies. My main interests include building PCs, 3D printing, playing video games, and going on hikes/backpacking trips.

I'm also a big Right to Repair advocate. I would much prefer to spend an afternoon troubleshooting hardware or repairing a few components rather than just throwing it away. Its a shame when companies create products in a way that discourages the customer to repair them, solely for a profit. If you want to see why that matters, check out iFixit.

Projects & Hands-On Work

Smart Calendar Integration

I'm currently building a smart-calendar system that links the Google Calendar API with Amazon Alexa. The goal is a hands-free schedule that I can update with ease. This is acting as a way to further learn about CSS, APIs and inter-device communication.

Hardware & Craftsmanship

I specialize in hardware troubleshooting, 3D printing, and woodworking. I find that the precision required for physical crafts translates directly to my work in computer science.
